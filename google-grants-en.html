Who is eligible?

To qualify for Google Ad Grants, your organization should meet these core requirements:

  • Be a registered nonprofit organization.
  • Be legally recognized as a charity or nonprofit in your country.
  • Have an active website that can be reviewed.

Not eligible: government entities, hospitals, schools or universities, political organizations, and for-profit businesses.

Frequently asked questions

Does unused credit roll over from the $10,000?

No. The program works on a "use it or lose it" basis. We do, however, help maximize your monthly spend.

Does the grant help with local outreach?

Yes, but the best results usually come from combining local targeting with broader reach.

How do we stay compliant with Google policies?

Our team handles the details and performs regular reviews to keep you within the rules.

What if Google rejects our application?

We review the reasons, make the required changes, and guide you through resubmission.

We already have a Google Ads account.

The grant account needs to be separate. We will help you create and configure it correctly.

Does the grant pay for RiseUp Solutions services?

No. The grant is only for ads. Our services are offered at discounted nonprofit-friendly rates.
