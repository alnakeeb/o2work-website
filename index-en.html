Did you know your organization can receive $140,000 annually in free digital grants?

Many nonprofits and charities are eligible for powerful digital grants from global tech companies like Google (up to $10,000/month) and Microsoft ($2,000/year). These grants help organizations expand their impact, reach more donors, and promote humanitarian projects online.

However, these grants require specific conditions

  • The organization must be registered as a nonprofit or charity.
  • It must own a professional, active website.
  • The website must be secure (SSL) and clearly state the mission and activities.
  • Must comply with the policies of the nonprofit grant programs.
  • Ad accounts and digital tools must be managed according to strict program guidelines.

Because of these requirements, many organizations rely on a specialized partner to apply for these grants, meet technical requirements, and manage accounts professionally.

RiseUp Solutions is a digital marketing agency dedicated to helping NGOs and nonprofits successfully apply for and manage these digital grants. From setting up your website and meeting technical requirements to managing ad campaigns and digital systems, we ensure your organization gets the most out of these powerful programs and comprehensive paid search.
