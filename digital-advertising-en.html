Frequently Asked Questions

Are digital ads necessary for nonprofits or just for businesses?

Digital advertising is just as essential for nonprofits as it is for businesses. Whether you aim to increase donations, promote events, or raise awareness, digital ads can help you achieve your goals effectively. In today's digital age, online visibility is crucial for attracting supporters and expanding your organization's impact.

How much will it cost? Do we have the budget?

The cost of digital advertising varies based on your organization's goals, chosen platforms, and target audience. At RiseUp Solutions, we understand budgets may be limited, so we work with you to create an affordable strategy that maximizes impact without straining resources. For a personalized estimate, we encourage you to contact us directly.

How much time and effort will be required from us?

Getting started requires some initial input from your team, but we handle the heavy lifting. We'll need your vision regarding your organization's mission, goals, voice, and visual identity. If you have existing copy and creative assets, we're happy to use them; if not, we can create them for you. Even if you have no prior experience, we're here to guide you.

How do we ensure we're spending wisely and not wasting money on ads?

We prioritize making every dollar count by implementing conversion tracking, ensuring your ad spend is directly tied to tangible results — whether leads, form submissions, event registrations, or donations. Our data-driven approach means we continuously monitor and optimize your campaigns to improve performance.

How long does a digital advertising campaign last?

Campaign duration depends on your organization's goals and needs. However, we typically recommend committing to at least a three-month campaign. This timeframe allows us to effectively optimize your ads, gather valuable data, and make necessary adjustments to ensure the best results. Longer campaigns often lead to stronger, more sustainable outcomes.

What's the difference between PPC and digital advertising?

There's essentially no difference between PPC and digital advertising — it's just a matter of terminology. PPC is a type of digital advertising where you pay each time someone clicks your ad. Digital advertising includes PPC along with other strategies like display ads, social media ads, and more. The goal is the same: delivering your message to the right audience.
